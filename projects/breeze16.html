Breeze16

Breeze16 is a 16x resource pack for Minecraft with many advanced features.

Soft, warm art style

Breeze aims to provide a cute and comfortable visual experience for the player

Extensive shaderpack compatibility

Breeze follows the LabPBR standard for emission and subsurface scattering, allowing for cool lighting effects

Builder's addon pack

An addon pack is available that includes many custom blocks and CIT models
